Baby BIO range by Saraya Goodmaid is a specially developed range of baby range products that is catered towards the delicate nature of baby’s health and wellbeing.

Enhanced with GREEN technology from Saraya Japan, Baby BIO is produced from the core values of Sanitation, Environment and Health.

Baby BIO range is FREE from:

  • Chemicals that are harmful or carcinogenic to human well-being.
  • Hormone Disruptive Chemicals.
  • Chemicals that have negative effects on marine ecology.
